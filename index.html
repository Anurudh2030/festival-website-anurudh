Upcoming Kerala Festivals

Christmas

Celebrated with great enthusiasm, featuring beautifully decorated churches, traditional carol singing, and special Christmas feasts across Kerala's Christian communities.

Onam

The most important and popular festival of Kerala, celebrating the harvest season with grand feasts, traditional games, and beautiful flower arrangements (Pookalam).

Vishu

Kerala's New Year festival, marked by the traditional Vishukkani (auspicious sight), giving of gifts, and preparing a special feast to bring prosperity for the coming year.

Thrissur Pooram

The most spectacular temple festival in Kerala, featuring magnificent elephant processions, traditional music, and spectacular fireworks in Thrissur.

Guruvayur Ekadasi

A significant religious festival at the famous Guruvayur Krishna Temple, attracting devotees from across Kerala for special prayers and celebrations.

Nehru Trophy Boat Race

The most popular of the boat races in Kerala, held annually during the Onam season, showcasing the incredible rowing skills and team spirit of Kerala.

About Kerala Festivals

Kerala's festivals are a vibrant reflection of its rich cultural heritage, blending traditions from various religions and communities. From the harvest celebrations of Onam to the spiritual fervor of temple festivals, each celebration showcases the unique spirit of God's Own Country. Our mission is to preserve, promote, and share the authentic experiences of Kerala's diverse festive traditions.
